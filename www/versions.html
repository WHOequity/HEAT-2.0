Versions

 

July 2017 – Version 2.0

  • Restructuring of source code for improved performance, including
    • Improving the cascade of user selections to minimize plot redrawing, and
    • Pre-calculating 95% confidence intervals of summary measures (instead of on the fly).
  • Addition of a new tab under Explore Inequality featuring detailed bar graphs.
  • Addition of accordion-style menus for graph options and table options.
  • Alteration of variable names in order to conform with the terminology used in HEAT Plus, including
    • Changing ‘country’ to ‘setting’, and
    • Changing ‘national average’ to ‘setting average’.
  • Revision of summary measures of inequality, including
    • Dropping the previous Relative Index of Inequality (RII),
    • Renaming the previous Kunst-Mackenbach Index (KMI) to Relative Index of Inequality (RII),
    • Revising the formulas for the Slope Index of Inequality (SII) and the Relative Index of Inequality (RII), both for the estimate and analytic 95% confidence intervals,
    • Adding the Weighted Index of Disparity (IDIS_W),
    • Dropping the bootstrap 95% confidence intervals,
    • Adding the simulation-based 95% confidence intervals for the Index of Disparity (IDIS), Mean Difference from Mean (MDM), Mean Difference from Best-Performing Subgroup (MDB) and Weighted Index of Disparity (IDIS_W),
    • Calculating the average as the weighted average based on the estimate and population size.

 

January 2017 - Version 1.1

  • Update of the built-in Health Equity Monitor database (2016 update).

 

May 2016 - Version 1.0

  • Initial public release.
